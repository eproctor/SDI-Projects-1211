SDI 1211: Eugene Proctor

Project 3

This story is a creative story about the average workday of a super hero.

The name of this super hero is Ultra Man. Ultra Man has the ability to phase shift

through walls and transform his atoms into diamonds. He recently discovered

his powers, so he hasn't discovered his weakness yet. By the end of this story,

maybe Ultra Man's weakness will be revealed.
